About Us

Grantham based cake studio Freelicious offers celebration cakes for any occasion. Our range includes traditional birthday cakes, cupcakes and letter cakes to make sure you will find the right fit for your special event. Operating from a purpose build kitchen that achieved five-star hygiene rating from local council, we also specialise in special dietary requirements so everybody can be catered for. Our environment is 100% gluten-free and other “free-from” friendly. Check out our offer of cakes and tray bakes or fill in the enquiry form. You can also follow us on social media and subscribe to our newsletter.
